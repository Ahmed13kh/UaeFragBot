Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to UAE-FragBot! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you interact with our web app.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Details you provide, such as preferences like gender, and queries.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interaction with the app (e.g., pages visited, timestamps).
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, and operating system.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide accurate perfume recommendations.
  • Improve the chatbot’s performance and user experience.
  • Personalize your interaction with UAE-FragBot.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share certain information as follows:

  • With OpenAI: Data entered in the chatbot may be shared with OpenAI’s ChatGPT API to process and generate accurate responses. This data is used solely for providing the chatbot functionality and is not stored by OpenAI or UAE-FragBot for long-term use.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To improve technical infrastructure using analytics tools.

By using the chatbot, you agree to the sharing of your input data with OpenAI for the purpose of generating responses.

4. Data Storage and Security

We do not store the data you provide in our systems. Your input is processed temporarily to provide recommendations and responses, and it is not retained after your session ends. Data shared with third-party services, such as OpenAI's ChatGPT API, is handled according to their data policies.

5. Cookies

Our web app does not use cookies to store or track user information. However, third-party services integrated into our app, such as OpenAI’s ChatGPT API, may process user data for functionality purposes. No persistent tracking or storage of user information is performed by our web app.

6. Your Rights

As a user of UAE-FragBot, you have the following rights regarding the data you provide:

  • Right to Transparency: You have the right to know what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used. We ensure transparency through this Privacy Policy.
  • Right to Access: You can request a summary of the data you have shared with us (e.g., preferences or queries) and how it has been processed. Contact us at ake179@student.bham.ac.uk to make such a request.
  • Right to Request Deletion: You may request that we delete any data you provided. Note that data shared with third-party APIs, like OpenAI's ChatGPT API, may not be retrievable or deletable.
  • Right to Limit Data Collection: You can limit the data you provide to only what is essential for using the app's features. This may restrict certain functionalities, such as personalized recommendations.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
